    Harvestr

    Hoping to reduce food waste, Harvestr connects people with a surplus of backyard produce. Users post fruits or veggies they want to trade, then specify what they want and a radius to search within. On this Tinder-style webapp, users swipe through potential trades, marking “yes” when they see something they want and “no” if they aren't interested. When there's a match, both users are notified via emails.

    Tech Stack: Flask, Python, CSS3, HTML5, Amazon S3, SQLAlchemy, Postgres, Oauth

    Deployed at: Harvestr
